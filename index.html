About Us

Haji Muhammad Rafique owned Riwaj Jewellers on February 12, 2000. With 43 years of experience and skill, he made the Oscar moments at Abu Dhabi as Z.N Jewellers. Along the way he is passionate and artist to work as a goldsmith and diamond expert. In 2000 he started jewellery shop as Riwaj in Cantt Sadar Bazar Sialkot.Since then he working hard with art to create the innovative and stylish touch of gems & diamonds along with precious gold. All his designs and styles being trendy and stylish let you rock on special moments.
